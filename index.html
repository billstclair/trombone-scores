Trombone Scores

This directory contains scores of trombone parts for the melodies of popular songs. I created them in Opusmodus. I started by writing the score in Opusmodus Notation (OMN), exporting as MusicXML and Midi, importing the MusicXML into MuseScore, saving in mcsz format, and printing to PDF. Later, I switched to doing the composing directly in MuseScore. I may switch back after I add midi input to Opusmodus.

Saved to GitHub as github.com/billstclair/trombone-scores.

MuseScore is a free and open source sheet music notation system.
