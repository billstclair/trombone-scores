Trombone Scores

This directory contains scores of trombone parts for the melodies of popular songs. I created them in Opusmodus. All were created by writing the score in Opusmodus Notation (OMN), exporting as MusicXML and Midi, importing the MusicXML into MuseScore, saving in mcsz format, and printing to PDF.

Saved to GitHub as github.com/billstclair/trombone-scores.

MuseScore is a free and open source sheet music notation system.
